Center for Research and Development of Engineering Technology
 Introduction
CRDET focuses on upgrading engineering technologies, improving the quality of engineering education and enhancing collaboration on technology development
 Establishment goal
  • Promote engineering, academic and technological exchanges inside and outside the school.
  • Promote the integration and practical application of engineering technology research expertise.
  • Assist the industry in research and development or industrial upgrading, and enhance the opportunities for industry-academic cooperation between our teachers and industry.
  • Assist and improve the domestic industry's ability to respond to the country's economic transformation in order to promote technological autonomy and localization of the industry.
  • Support and coordinate related teaching research and promotion of various departments.
  • Other matters related to the research and development of the School of Engineering.
 Direction of development
  • To undertake such as college-level or inter-academic-level work projects.
  • Assist in the promotion and counseling of research plans and talent training conferences with manufacturers in the Central Science Park.
  • Strengthen the cooperation and interaction between the hospital and industrial unions, industrial parks, and science park manufacturers.
  • Handle key scientific and technological forums such as electrical machinery, optoelectronics, communications, precision machinery, biomedical engineering, disaster prevention, and nanotechnology, and actively host domestic and foreign conferences.
  • Enhance opportunities for industry-university cooperation between teachers of our college and industry, strive for "local value-added academic disciplines" and cross-field cooperation, and promote the integration and practicality of engineering technology.
